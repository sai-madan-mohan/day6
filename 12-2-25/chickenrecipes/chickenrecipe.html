Ingredients :

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 onions (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1-inch ginger (grated)
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instruction :

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions until golden.
  2. Add garlic, ginger, and spices, and cook for a minute.
  3. Add chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Pour in coconut milk and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, then serve hot with rice.
