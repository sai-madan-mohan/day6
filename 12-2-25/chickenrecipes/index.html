🐔Preparing spicy chicken🐔

Ingredients

🍗 Chicken
🧅 Onions
🍅 Tomatoes
🧄 Garlic
🌿 Spices
💧Oil
🧂 Salt

Instructions

1. Cut the chicken into pieces.
2. Chop onions and tomatoes.
3. Heat oil in a pan.
4. Add garlic and ginger paste.
5. Cook onions until golden brown.
6. Add tomatoes and cook well.
7. Add chicken and spices.
8. Simmer for 20 minutes.
9. Serve hot with rice!
