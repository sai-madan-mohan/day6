Idly Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Idly Rice
  • 1 cup Urad Dal (Black Gram)
  • 1 tsp Fenugreek Seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak the idly rice and urad dal separately for 4-6 hours.
  2. Soak the fenugreek seeds along with the urad dal.
  3. Drain the water and grind the urad dal and fenugreek seeds into a smooth batter using a grinder or blender. Add water as needed.
  4. Grind the idly rice coarsely and mix it with the urad dal batter.
  5. Add salt to the batter and mix well. The consistency of the batter should be slightly thicker than dosa batter.
  6. Let the batter ferment overnight or for 8-10 hours in a warm place.
  7. Grease idly molds with a little oil and pour the batter into the molds.
  8. Steam the idlis for 10-12 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  9. Allow the idlis to cool for a few minutes, then remove them from the molds.
  10. Serve hot with sambar and coconut chutney.
